Programme structure

Five pillars, five weekends

Each weekend explores one pillar of holistic wellbeing

01
Whenua — Foundations
30 Jul–2 Aug 2026

Yoga history, anatomy basics, the structure of safe asana, and introducing yourself as a teacher.

02
Tinana — Body
20–23 Aug 2026

Deep-dive anatomy, biomechanics, applied physiology, and intelligent alignment in asana.

Yoga philosophy, the Yoga Sutras, Gita, meditation, and the psychology of teaching.

04
Wairua — Spirit
15–18 Oct 2026

05
Whānau — Community
12–15 Nov 2026

Practicum, peer teaching, ethics, and stepping into the world as a certified teacher.

Foundation Teacher Training

Begin your
teaching journey.

Our flagship 200hr Vinyasa YTT is a comprehensive journey into the art and science of yoga teaching. Structured around five weekends, each intensive explores one of the five pillars of Te Whare Tapa Whā — the Māori model of holistic health developed by Sir Mason Durie.

This training is equally powerful for those who want to become teachers and those who simply want to deepen their own practice. Either way, you'll leave transformed.

Complete all five intensives and earn your 200hr certificate from AWHI Yoga School, qualifying you for Yoga Alliance RYT 200 registration.
