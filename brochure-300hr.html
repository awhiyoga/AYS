Advanced Teacher Training

Go further.
Teach deeper.

Building on your 200hr foundation, this advanced programme takes you deeper into teaching methodology, personal practice, and the rich intersection of yoga philosophy and Te Ao Māori wisdom.

Structured as standalone modules with a hybrid learning format — in-person intensives combined with online content and monthly live wānanga — you can join for a single module, collect CE credits, or complete the full 300-hour certification.

Complete all required modules and two electives to earn your 300hr certificate from AWHI Yoga School and qualify for Yoga Alliance RYT 500 registration.

Three ways to join

Deepen your practice — most modules open to all, no certification required
Earn CE credits — for 200hr+ teachers maintaining Yoga Alliance registration
Full 300hr certification — pathway to RYT 500 for 200hr CYT holders

Our training honours Te Whare Tapa Whā — weaving ancient yoga teachings with Māori ways of knowing and being.
