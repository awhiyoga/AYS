Our kōrero

A space where every body is welcome

At AWHI, we've built a studio where genuine connection, mindful movement, and community sit at the heart of everything we do.

Guided by Te Whare Tapa Whā, we honour the four dimensions of wellness: body, mind, spirit, and community a holistic approach to wellbeing.

Whatever brings you to the mat, you'll find your place here.

Our philosophy
Our Kaupapa

Four pillars of wellbeing

Everything starts with individual wellness. This leads to better relationships, a clearer purpose, and a deeper connection to the world around us.

01
Tinana

Body

Through mindful movement we create physical health — building strength, flexibility, and a lasting relationship with our bodies.

02
Hinengaro

Mind

Through mindfulness practices we develop emotional awareness and the capacity to navigate life with clarity and grace.

03
Wairua

Spirit

Physical and mental practice can connect us to something beyond the individual — a sense of purpose and belonging.

04
Whānau

Community

We grow together. A supportive environment where collective wellbeing is as important as individual progress.

Our Whare

More than a studio — a whare, a home

Located on Level 2, 15 Johnston Street in Wellington CBD, AWHI is designed to feel like a genuine refuge in the city.

Two purpose-built spaces — Whare Oho for our main classes, and Whare Wānanga for intimate sessions and teacher training — plus a community lounge where the kettle is always on.
