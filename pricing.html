Good to know

Membership Terms

Key things to know before joining. Full terms available on request.

Minimum commitment

Direct debit memberships require a 3-month minimum (minimum spend $414, or $330 concession). After that, your membership rolls month-to-month automatically.

Cancellation

To cancel, email kiaora@awhiyoga.co.nz at least 14 days before your next payment date. Memberships cannot be cancelled before the 3-month minimum has been reached.

Pausing your membership

Pauses are available for a minimum of 2 consecutive weeks and a maximum of 8 consecutive weeks per 3-month contract. 14 days' written notice required. No retroactive holds.

Prepaid & class passes

Prepaid memberships (6- and 12-month) and class passes are non-refundable and non-transferable. 10-class passes are valid for 6 months; casual passes are valid for 1 month from purchase.
