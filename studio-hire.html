Booking & Terms

How to Reserve
Our Studio

Once studio availability has been confirmed, please read, complete and return the Hire Agreement to reserve your booking.

Hire Only — Payment & Holding

Payment is due within 48 hours of your event or booking date.

We will hold your booking for free unless another party requests the studio on your reserved date. If a competing enquiry is received, you will need to pay your hire fee (non-refundable) in full within 3 days to hold the space.

If you decide not to proceed at that point, the booking will be offered to the other enquiring party.

Hire + Promo — Payment Terms

  • 50% deposit is required before any promotions are made or posted, to cover marketing staff costs. This deposit is non-refundable on cancellation.
  • A minimum of 4 weeks lead time is required to promote your event. Promotional support cannot be provided if marketing materials (images, description, bio, etc.) are not received in time.
  • The remaining balance is due within 48 hours of your event or booking date.
